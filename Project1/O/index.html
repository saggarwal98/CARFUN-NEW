MAHINDRA TUV300 PLUS

With many first-in-segment features and the fluidic 2.0 design philosophy, this car is a built to make everyday an adventure.

REVIEW

Mahindra TUV300 Plus distinguishes itself from a regular version with a nine seater layout, more features and a larger diesel engine from the Scorpio. The SUV is available in three variants - P4, P6 and the P8. Further, it comes with five colour options such as - bold black, majestic silver, dynamo red, glacier white and molten orange.
The TUV300 Plus is essentially a lengthened version of the TUV300 sub-four-meter compact SUV. It shares most of the body panels with the standard model but comes with unique rear styling. The base variant comes with an engine putting out 100bhp@3750rpm and 240Nm@1600-2800rpm of max power and max torque respectively. The gasoline motor comes mated to 5 Speed Manual transmission which is quite a joy to use. Mahindra claims an average of 18.5 kmpl for this mid T6 Plus variant.




Styling
The SUV now measures 4,400mm in length, 1,835mm in width and stands at a height of 1,812mm. The width remains unchanged at 1,835mm, while the height has been reduced to 1,812mm with a marginal drop of five millimeter. The SUV gets reshaped headlamps with a lighter tint, redesigned bumpers, smaller fog lamps flanked by new silver inserts and larger 16-inch wheels. The SUV also gets a pair of new boxy, wraparound tail lamps, a smaller roof-mounted spoiler and also body colour spare wheel cover.The TUV300 Plus will be sold with four exterior paint shade options, however, the P4 variant has been seen in the white shade only, as of now. The new model looks more proportionate and stylish than the regular model. Though, overall styling of the SUV looks largely identical to the regular TUV300. The stretched wheelbase allows Mahindra to create extra space inside the cabin.


Performance
The TUV300 Plus, as reported by us earlier, is powered by a 120hp 2.2-litre diesel engine which Mahindra calls as mHawk120. It generates 280Nm of torque. Gearbox duties are done by a six-speed manual instead of the five-speed unit in the standard TUV300. The vehicle comes with 215/70R16 tyres with steel wheels. It gets a 60-litre fuel tank and weighs 2,410kg. The TUV300 Plus also comes with Mahindra’s fuel-saving Micro Hybrid tech.This engine comes with a 6-speed manual transmission and also benefitted with Micro Hybrid start-stop system.Suspension mechanism of the Mahindra TUV300 Plus comprises of double wishbone with coil spring and multilink unit with coil spring at the front and rear, respectively. For braking, the SUV gets front disc and rear drum brakes.
Comfort & Quality
The new Mahindra TUV300 Plus gets faux leather seats, a 17.8 cm touchscreen infotainment with GPS navigation and Pininfarina design theme. Moreover, the SUV is now available with ECO Mode, Micro Hybrid technology, brake energy regeneration, intellipark reverse assist, AC ECO mode and driver information system. It also gets a steering mounted audio and phone controls, a rear defogger along with wash and wipe, a driver seat height adjuster, armrests for the front row, a convenient storage tray below the driver’s seat, lead-me-to-vehicle and follow-me-home headlamps.Like the exterior, the interior design and features of the car is similar to the smaller sibling. It gets dual-tone black and beige interior theme with piano black finish on the centre console. Instead of jumper seats behind the second row, the new TUV300 Plus sports forward-facing third row of seats. The new model comes with 9-seat layout. It gets multi-information display, automatic climate control with rear AC vents, multi-functional steering wheel, etc.



Safety
Robust built with high strength steel provides toughness and power to the structure. Anyi Lock Bracking System along with Eletronic Brake Force Distribution and Cornering Brake Control ensure precise braking in cornering conditions.Dual airbags provide maximum protection to the driver and the co-passenger.
The advanced encoded key recognition system rejects all keys except its own, providing enhanced protection against theft.Automatic Door Lock avoids accidental opening doors.
Rear Defoggers help keep a clear view through the windshield with heating elements which defog the class electrically.

The new 17.8 cm touchscreen infotainment system includes wireless music and audio calling technologies via GPS,Bluetooth, USB video and image playback,Bluesense Technology, a driver information system and comes connected to steering mounted audiio controlspaired with 4 speakers and 2 tweeters.
Micro hybrid technology switches the engine off when it's idle for 2 seconds and switches back on as soon as clutch pedal is pressed. It leadsw to better fuel economy and reduced emission.
Voice messaging system provides convenient voice alert for seat belt warnins,door-ajar alerts,engaged handbrakes.
The TUV300 Plus comes with Intellipad Reverse Assist for ease of parking. The smart driver information system keeps updayed with vital ehicle information such as azverage fuel efficiency, distance to empty, service information and more for a hassle free experience.
