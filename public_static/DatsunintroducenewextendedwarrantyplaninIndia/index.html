- Extended warranty of five years with unlimited kilometers
- New package to offer five standard services
- Part of Datsun's fifth anniversary celebrations in India
Datsun has introduced new offers for customers to celebrate its fifth anniversary in India. These include a new extended warranty plan and a ‘Peace of Mind’ package. Anyone buying the Go, Go Plus or the Redi-Go will be offered a five years/unlimited km warranty. This five-year warranty with no limit on kilometers includes coverage for engine, AC Compressor, alternator, ECU, shock absorber etc. The 'Peace of Mind' plan packs five standard services with every Datsun car. These include 24x7 road-side assistance, free pick-up and drop facilities to workshop, free insurance and a special scheme for Government employees both in service and retired. Another advantage is that this package is transferrable to the new owner, in case the car is sold to someone else. All of these new schemes are available at all Nissan-Datsun touch points across the country.
